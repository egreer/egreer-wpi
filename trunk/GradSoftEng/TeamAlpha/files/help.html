How to Play
1- Click on a cell and use your keyboard to enter numbers.
2- Click on a cell and press the delete or backspace to delete a value in the cell.
3- Each row and column should contian different values that are not repeated.
4- Each section (highlighted in bold) should contain the different values as well.
5- Secondary click or Ctrl+m to be in mark mode.
6- Primary click Ctrl+d to be in digit mode.
